Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

1. Website visitors

This website does not collect personal data. There are no accounts, no forms, no cookies and no analytics trackers.

2. Our publishing tool

Our internal studio tool connects to our own official social media accounts (such as TikTok) through the official platform APIs using OAuth. Access tokens are stored securely on our own devices, are used exclusively to upload our own original videos to our own accounts, and are never shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.

3. Third-party users

The tool is not offered to the public. It does not access, collect, store, or process data about any users other than our own accounts.

4. Data retention and deletion

We can revoke tool access at any time from the connected account settings, which invalidates its tokens. For any privacy request, contact us and we will respond promptly.
